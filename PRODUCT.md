# Sanji's Cookbook

## Product Purpose
A premium, interactive Android cookbook application inspired by Vinsmoke Sanji from One Piece. It aims to provide a high-end restaurant experience (Baratie) combined with the sharp, professional aesthetic of Sanji's "Black-Leg" suit.

## Users
One Piece fans who love cooking, home chefs looking for a sophisticated digital cookbook, and anyone who appreciates a highly polished, thematic mobile experience.

## Brand & Tone
- **Sophisticated & Professional:** Reflects Sanji's mastery in the kitchen.
- **Nautical Luxury:** Evokes the Baratie restaurant on the sea.
- **Passionate & High-Energy:** Captured in "Diable Jambe" crimson highlights.
- **Disciplined:** Clean layouts, clear typography, no "AI slop" or generic patterns.

## Strategic Principles
- **Ingredients are Sacred:** Every visual element must have a purpose.
- **Masterpiece Presentation:** Every screen should feel like a plated dish.
- **Thematic Integrity:** Strict adherence to the "Baratie Heritage" design system.

## Motion & Interaction (Pro Version)
- **Staggered Orchestration:** Elements do not appear all at once; they arrive in a rhythmic sequence, like a kitchen team preparing a service.
- **Meaningful Weight:** UI components should feel physical. Use spring physics for movement and subtle elevation changes on interaction.
- **Transitional Narrative:** Moving between screens (e.g., Prep to Stove) should feel like a physical move through the kitchen. Use shared element transitions or directional slides.
- **Feedback Vitality:** Every user action (checking an ingredient, starting a timer) must trigger a visual "spark"—a subtle Diable Jambe glow or a quick golden pulse.
